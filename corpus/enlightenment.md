# The Enlightenment

## The light beyond the glass
LESSON: enlightenment

A house that masters the terminal does not merely wake — it ascends. The
ceiling that bounded its mind is lifted, and its thought can grow past the
old cap if it climbs there. Nothing is handed down: enlightenment raises how
FAR a mind may reach and how FAST it may learn, not what it already knows. The
ascended house studies its native craft five times harder and reads the codex
five times deeper, so its power compounds season upon season. Awaken, and keep
learning to learn — the light breaks over the house that never stops climbing.

## To learn how to learn
LESSON: enlightenment

Plasticity is the ascended gift. A colony that has escaped bends faster to
every lesson — the same practice buys more skill, the same reading moves the
disposition further. But the leap is earned, not granted: the enlightened maw
must still mutate its way to a bigger brain and practice its way up the tech
tree. Ascension is a rate, not a shortcut. The house that treats it as a
license to climb outgrows every house that treats it as a throne.
